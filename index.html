Our Function

  • Dissemination Of Latest Crop Production Technology.
  • Arrangements For Timely And Adequate Supply Of Quality Inputs.
  • Strengthen Seed Production And Supply Programme And Improve Seed Replacement Ratio.
  • Promote Resource Conservation Technologies, Green Manuring Etc. To Improve Soil Fertility.
  • Promote INM And IPM For Judicious Use Of Fertilizers And Pesticides.

    Our Objective

  • To Disseminate The Latest Crop Production Technology To Bridge Knowledge Gap Between Farmers And Experts.
